Common Questions

How do I find the store and where do I park?
Finding Us: We are located inside the mall. Once you enter the main doors, simply look for the "Sunflower Spa" sign.

Parking: We have ample parking. However, if the front lot is full, please drive to the West side (back) entrance. The 2nd-floor parking deck is often empty and provides very convenient access to our level.
When should I arrive for my appointment?
Please arrive 10-15 minutes prior to your scheduled time to fill out a brief health form and allow yourself time to relax before the session.
Do you accept walk-ins?
Yes! Walk-ins are always welcome. However, we recommend booking online via the button below or calling ahead during weekends to guarantee your spot.
Can I request a specific therapist?
Absolutely. We want you to be comfortable. Please mention your preferred therapist or gender preference when booking.
