Common Questions

When should I arrive for my appointment?
Please arrive 10-15 minutes prior to your scheduled time to fill out a brief health form and allow yourself time to relax before the session.
Do you accept walk-ins?
Yes! Walk-ins are always welcome (Service Policy). However, we recommend booking online or calling ahead during weekends to guarantee your spot.
Is there parking available?
Yes, there is ample parking available at our location on Old Country Road.
Can I request a specific therapist?
Absolutely. We want you to be comfortable. Please mention your preferred therapist or gender preference when booking.
